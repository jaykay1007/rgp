Frequently Asked Questions About School Diary Printing

When should we place our school diary order?

We recommend placing your order at least 8-10 weeks before you need the diaries. For academic year diaries, most schools order in April-May for delivery before the new academic year in June.

What is the minimum order quantity for school diaries?

Our standard minimum order is 100 diaries, though we offer the most competitive pricing for orders of 500+ diaries. Special arrangements can be made for smaller quantities with adjusted pricing.

Can we use our own diary design or do you provide templates?

We offer both options. You can provide your own complete design, work with our designers to create a custom layout, or choose from our range of pre-designed templates that can be customized with your school's information.

What binding options are available for school diaries?

We offer several binding options including spiral binding (most popular for student diaries), perfect binding, saddle stitching, wire-o binding, and hardcover binding. Each has different durability and cost implications.

Can diaries be personalized for individual students?

Yes, we offer variable data printing allowing for personalization with student names, classes, admission numbers, and other details. This service has additional costs depending on the complexity of personalization.

What paper quality do you recommend for school diaries?

For student diaries, we typically recommend 80-100 GSM paper for interior pages which balances durability with cost-effectiveness. Cover pages are usually 250-300 GSM with lamination for added durability.
